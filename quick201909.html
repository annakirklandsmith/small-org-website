Programs and Group Needs

Don't you want to have the privilege of being the official founding treasurer of the Data Scientista Society, the most fabulous pile of woman brain in the world? No accounting background is necessary, and we have many advisors for orientation to the tasks of collecting donations (monthly), event sales (as needed), keeping the books, and doing the taxes (annually). This is a linchpin position so that the Society can become a 501(c)(3) organization. A deserving, synergized, co-supportive, talented leadership panel that 300+ women depend on awaits your inquiry to datascientistasociety@gmail.com.

Upcoming Events
Friday, September 20 and Saturday, September 21 NC Open Pass Civic Camp

Civic Camp is an annual civic tech event that brings citizens, government change agents, and businesses together to openly innovate and improve our communities. This event includes a project pitch for data projects about community improvement. That's broad; see examples here Following Civic Camp, teams work together and present at DataPalooza. NC Open Pass is a series of events connected to a nation-wide volunteer effort called Code for America

Monday, September 30 Data Scientista Society Book Club

We will be discussing Invisible Women: Data Bias in a World Designed for Men by Caroline Criado Perez. We are very excited that Lauren is hosting us in her home.

October 13, 14, and 15 All Things Open

All Things Open is the largest open source/open tech/open web conference on the East Coast of the United States. Google Cloud has given the Data Scientista Society a few free tickets. Reach out to datascientistasociety@gmail.com for the registration code.

October 28 Data Scientista Reverse Book Club

At Reverse Book Club, each person will share about a non-fiction book she has read.

November 8 and 9 WITS Southeast

The Women in Technology Summit is a woman-only event. This one is our jam! Probably the main part of the conference is on Saturday.

November 14 DataPalooza

NC Open Pass DataPalooza is an open data competition to catalyze positive community and economic impact with coaching and cash prizes. We hope we will be there to cheer for an official Data Scientista Society contestant.
