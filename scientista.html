What is a data scientista?

We, the members of the Data Scientista Society, are the definers of what a data scientista is. But you guessed correctly already: it's a friendly blend of "data scientist" and "sister." We are here to support one another. The true purpose of having our own concept is so that we can be as inclusive of as many kinds of data-related careers as possible. We've got women who:

  • women who are curious about the field
  • women with PhDs
  • women who code
  • women who don't code
  • women who use propietary tools
  • women with degrees from community colleges
  • women who majored in music but now work with data
  • women who aren't on the technical side, but support a data project, in, say, marketing
  • women backgrounds in math or statistics
  • women who associate themselves easily with a data buzzwordm
  • women who want to know what a data buzzword actually means
  • women who are looking for a data job
  • women who can hire people on data their teams
  • women backgrounds in philosophies like Lean and Six Sigma
  • women who might be called stay-at-home but who are data-driven
  • women trained in library science or linguistics
  • women of every age and career stage
  • women who are self-employed
  • women in transition with their lives and their careers

If you have an interest in data, but you don't feel anything on that list describes you, plese reach out, and we will stretch it.
