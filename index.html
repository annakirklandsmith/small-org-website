imagine a world full of data of the women, by the women, for the women

The Data Scientista Society (formerly Data + Women + Triangle) builds relationships among women in analytics, statistics, and data science (we call these women data scientistas) through the scientistas themselves teaching one another about data and sharing their enthusiasm in various casual and collaborative formats.

The scientistas are a community and network of women in the Triangle of North Carolina (Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill) seeking to foster discussions of unique to analytical women that will ultimately lead to efforts that empower women in the data industry through tangible initiatives.
