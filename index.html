The Data Scientista Society

The mission of the Data Scientista Society is to build relationships among co-located women pursuing careers in data science, a.k.a data scientistas, while also practicing mutual supportiveness towards competency, success, and leadership.

The data scientistas themselves teach one another about data and share their enthusiasm in various casual and collaborative formats. They seek to foster discussions unique to analytical women that will ultimately lead to efforts that empower women in the data industry through tangible initiatives. Here are the values that guide us in our activities.

Gratitude

We are thankful for one another, for opportunties, and we are willing to leverage available resources.

Grit

Grit, according to Angela Duckworth, means we perservere with our passion: data.

Growth Mindset

With a growth mindset, according to Carol Dweck, we can improve at anything, including, the skill of supporting other women.

Grip

We focus on our strengths, and affirm the perspective of our background.

Grapes of Empathy

We are ethical and world-improving with what we do. We strive the study and understand the impact that data leaves on society.

Gravity

We are solution-oriented, as it isn't enough to define a problem.

Great Women

We are inspired by and rely on the examples of women who have gone before us; we treasure mentorships and partnerships.

Inclusion

Inclusion doesn't start with gr, but notice we include it anyway. There are all kinds of women with all kinds of data careers. Everyone is welcome, for any amount of time. We are grateful for your time!
