Future Fair

Three Nights For Local Tech Women at All Career Levels

Any tech women who needs to strategize how to find her next chapter needs the latest advice about job searching. But even if she is happy in her job, a woman near her needs support. So, what can we learn so that we support other women?

Featured Speaker, Jessica Mitsch

Jessica Mitch is the CEO of Momentum Learning, a code school with a 6-month money back guarantee. Momentum Learning transitions over 75 adults per year to be developers not only through code skills training, but also attitude and career skills training. As someone who shoulders the responsbility of placing adults with all kinds backgrounds into the tech world, Jessica's advice is the latest and greatest and often targeted to folks who haven't always had it easy. Come hear the same advice Jessica gives to the students at Momentum Learning.
