Beginnings

The Data Scientista Society began as Data+Women+Triangle, as the invitation to start the group was originally from Tableau. The kickoff meeting was on May 23, 2016, and had 30 attendees. However, analytic groups in the Triangle abounded. Most groups were "sage on stage" style. But is that what makes women feel supported? No, women bond through eye-contact. Our challenge was to develop an organization that would retain women in data science.

Nowadays

Ever since, we've met at least once a month, except in the month of December. So after three years, we have had over 50 meetings, and we have over 400 members of our Meetup site.
